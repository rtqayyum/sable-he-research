# Proof obligations

The proof obligations are tracked by `sable.proofs.proof_obligations()` and cover:

1. compact sparse-LPN encryption correctness;
2. GSW-style scalar encryption invariant;
3. expansion correctness;
4. addition/multiplication invariant;
5. compaction correctness;
6. compaction-key replacement;
7. expansion-key replacement;
8. challenge-ciphertext replacement.
