# HE.org, NIST, ISO, and Certification Positioning

HomomorphicEncryption.org is the natural community context for HE discussion and security guidelines, but SABLE-HE is not an HE.org standard. NIST PQC FIPS 203/204/205 standards cover KEM/signature functionality rather than homomorphic encryption. FIPS 140-3/CMVP validation applies to cryptographic modules and approved/recommended algorithms through external lab validation.

Therefore the SABLE-HE claim should remain:

> ready for external cryptanalysis and pre-standardization discussion.

not:

> standardized, certified, or production-approved cryptography.
