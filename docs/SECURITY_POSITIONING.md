# Security Positioning

Use this wording consistently:

> SABLE-HE Research is a post-quantum code/LPN-based homomorphic-encryption research implementation. Its post-quantum character is conditional on the assumed hardness of sparse q-ary LPN and q-ary code/LPN decoding-style problems against classical and quantum adversaries.

Avoid these claims:

- certified production cryptography;
- NIST-approved homomorphic encryption;
- secure for sensitive production data;
- replacement for audited HE libraries;
- full FHE.

Acceptable claims:

- public research implementation;
- candidate post-quantum code-based HE construction;
- leveled HE for low-degree arithmetic;
- encrypted additive aggregation and FedAvg experiments;
- open for independent cryptanalysis.
